SPF 90 FPS

Spend a white-hot Toronto summer exploring the wondrous, playful and comedic possibilities of virtual reality.

DMG's intensive programs give a small group of genderqueer, nonbinary, two spirit, femmes, and women space and one-on-one support to focus on expanding their artistic practice with game, interactive and experiential design.

Over the course of 6 weeks, you will take your concept from idea to interactive VR vignette, with 40 hours of classroom instruction, one-on-one mentor support, access to the latest VR hardware and software, workspace, presentation and playtesting opportunities. The program will culminate in a public exhibition at our new gallery space.

Application Process

Applications will be accepted on a rolling basis from May 8–June 20, or until all spots are filled.

There is no cost to apply. The enrollment fee for selected applicants is $250.

Please apply even if you're not sure you can afford the fee. We'll work something out!

All materials and software will be provided. Bring your own laptop if possible – but we have loaners as well as desktop workstations available by reservation.

Who This Program is For

This program is for:

  • Toronto-based genderqueer, nonbinary, two spirit, femme folks and/or women over the age of 18.
  • Creators who wish to explore the expressive potential of interactive VR experiences
  • Animators, filmmakers, game designers, video artists, sound artists, performing artists, fashion designers, world builders!

While comfort with computers and basic programming concepts will be an asset, no coding/programming, game, VR or art experience is required. Be ready to roll up your sleeves! We'll be diving into intermediate concepts, but you'll have plenty of support through our experienced instructors and fleet of friendly mentors.
