Bit Bazaar, Toronto's festival of indie games, DIY comics & zines is bringing its 8th edition to The Ex!

◉ August 25-27, 10 a.m.-10 p.m.

◉ CNE Grounds • Gaming Garage • Enercare Centre, Hall F

◉ Free with CNE admission! ($15 through Aug 17)

About

A videogame arts & crafts market + zine fair

Play minty-fresh games! Buy one-of-a-kind, limited-edition and handmade/DIY items—pins, zines, boxed games, card decks, art prints, posters, comics, t-shirts, game soundtracks on vinyl, mixtapes, papercraft and toys—direct from 50+ independent game makers!

Apply to Exhibit

  • Applications are open until July 31.
  • There is no fee to apply or to exhibit.
  • It's an indoor, air-conditioned venue.
  • All exhibitors get one 6-foot table, and are required to staff your booth through at least one 6-hour shift (10am-4pm or 4pm-10pm).
  • Games exhibitors: Video games, board games, card games, tabletop games, artist collectives, interactive things and VR experiences are all welcome. Bring a complete version or playable demo plus one-of-a-kind, handmade/DIY items, boxed games, or other merch to sell.
  • Comics, books + zines exhibitors: Self-published books, comics, and zines; webcomics, small press, collectives, illustrators. Bring all of your merch – posters, pins, novels, collects, totes, etc.!
