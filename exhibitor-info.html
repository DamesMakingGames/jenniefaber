About

We are so excited you're part of this special (eighth!) edition of Bit Bazaar, happening at the Canadian National Exhibition August 25-27. The CNE is a large, public event and we're thrilled to be part of the CNE's inaugural Gaming Garage alongside triple A studios, esports, pinball, and a board game area. We expect about 40,000 people per day visiting the Gaming Garage.

Insurance

You are required to have insurance coverage for your booth. We have purchased this upfront and split the cost across all Bit Bazaar exhibitors, and each exhibitor will need to chip in $18.

Badges

We'll notify you when they're ready for pick up at Gamma Space (862 Richmond Street West, Suite 100).

Each exhibitor will receive 2 badges. If you have additional staff for the same shift, you’ll need to purchase a general admission badge ($15 until Aug. 17; $19 thereafter) for them.

Badges don’t have names on them, so you can share them with different people across different days.

Restricted Goods

✅  Stickers are okay! The CNE has made a special exception to allow Bit Bazaar exhibitors to have stickers at their table.

However, they must be offered for sale – you may not give them away for free. We recommend they also be packaged (clear plastic bags work great). We will be fined if stickers from any exhibitor are found stuck on the grounds.

🚫  Toys, children’s clothing, cosmetics, and food items are not allowed for sale at your booth. And no raffles or contests.

Tables

  • Tables will be skirted, but you may bring your own table cloth.
  • Power will be provided, but we recommend bringing your own power bar just in case!
  • If you're sharing a table with another exhibitor, please do not bring a monitor larger than 27".
  • Two chairs per half table, and four per full table will be provided. P

Content

If your game, art or comic contains content not suitable for all ages, please be conscientious of the public nature of this event and provide a content warning, and ensure any adult content is not easily visible to passersby.

Move-In Procedure

All move-in – of large items, multiple loads, or anything that requires a dolly – must be done through the Manitoba Drive entrance of the Exhibition grounds. This entrance is located on the east side of the grounds running off Strachan Avenue and Fleet Street (North of the Princes’ Gates).

You can access the Enercare Centre through Door #33 off of Manitoba Drive, Hall F, East Annex.

Move-In Hours

Move in is only allowed during the following time slots:

Day Move-In Window
Thursday, August 24 8:00 a.m.-6:00 p.m.
Friday, August 25 8:00 a.m.-9:45 a.m.
Saturday, August 26 8:00 a.m.-9:45 a.m.
Sunday, August 27 8:00 a.m.-9:45 a.m.

Only doing an afternoon slot? Here are some options:

  • Drop off your goods/equipment ahead of time during one of the slots listed above, and tuck them under your table until your shift.
  • Carry your stuff through the main entrance in one load. However, you cannot use a dolly or load in any large items (including monitors or computers) this way
  • Coordinate with someone tabling before you to borrow a monitor.
